Dover Artificial Lift is a group of leading artificial lift and surface production brands brought together because of a common desire to provide our clients with the best production solutions in the oil and gas industry.


At its heart, Dover Artificial Lift is both a discrete and integrated solutions provider with expertise and reliable products in the artificial lift and surface production spectrums. Our products are delivered to nearly every major oil and gas market globally through sales offices, representatives or distribution. Manufacturing facilities in North America, Australia and Middle East supply the global market with the highest quality and most reliable products and solutions available.


Dover Artificial Lift brands have spent years developing and innovating their manufacturing processes to ensure the highest levels of quality. We have a shared customer-driven focus and commitment to delivering increased returns, reduced operating costs, and enhanced safety for people and the environment to oil and gas production operations.
