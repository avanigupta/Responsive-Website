Product Software


In March 2003, Dover started an R&D facility- India Innovation Centre (IIC) in Bangalore and became the first global R&D facility for several Dover operating companies. Ever since, IIC has been a great resource of engineering talent, leadership and innovation for Dover.


IIC Product software teams are an integral corporate arm providing value to Dover Operating Companies and its customers through technology, product and business solutions, thus meeting global standards of quality and innovation.


Product software teams at IIC comprises of cross-functional teams of software, firmware, testing, electrical and mechanical engineers involved in building complete products and solutions. The products that we build include SCADA systems, HMI/GUI, RTU host data communications, Industrial automation and control systems, standard Fieldbus control systems, Fuel management and control systems.


The Product development teams at IIC over the years have built the domain knowledge in the areas of Oil and Gas, Semiconductor - surface mount technology, heat exchangers.

Currently, Ferguson Beauregard, Multitest, OPWFMS, SWEP, Theta Oil Field Services and Waukesha teams are successfully delivering and supporting products from IIC for over 6 years and these teams now function as an extended R&D arm of their respective operating companies.
